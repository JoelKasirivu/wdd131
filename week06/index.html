Welcome to In His Hands

We are a community-driven organization focused on uplifting lives through education, health, and empowerment.

Michelle Delhaute McGowan Organization president Michelle started In His Hands after God called her to do something about the orphan crisis. The work of In His Hands has been life-changing for Michelle as she has had the blessing of seeing transformation in orphaned children who have been part of the ministry. She loves helping orphans and spending time with her family. Michelle and her husband are the parents of 9 children, 4 of whom were adopted from China. She travels to the countries we work in to spend time with the children, get to know people in the community and plan programs.
